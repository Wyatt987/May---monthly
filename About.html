About the Knicks

About the 2024–25 Knicks

The Knicks return for the 2024–25 NBA season with high expectations after a strong postseason run. Building on their defensive identity and chemistry, the team now looks poised to contend for a top spot in the Eastern Conference. This year’s squad blends All-Star firepower, youthful energy, and one of the league’s most respected coaching staffs. All eyes are on Madison Square Garden as the team sets its sights on a championship chase.

Knicks Offseason & Outlook

During the offseason, the Knicks focused on bolstering their bench depth and securing key extensions for core players. Strategic additions aim to support their star duo with more consistent shooting and veteran leadership. Head coach Tom Thibodeau continues to emphasize grit and accountability, while younger players are expected to take on expanded roles. The organization remains committed to long-term growth and short-term success.

Core Players

Jalen Brunson

The Knicks’ floor leader and one of the most efficient point guards in the league. A consistent scoring and playmaking threat.

Josh Hart

An all-around glue guy, Hart brings defense, hustle, and leadership. A fan favorite and team-first mentality on display every night.

Karl-Anthony Towns

A dominant forward with elite scoring and rebounding ability. A key veteran presence in the frontcourt.

Coaching Staff

Tom Thibodeau

Known for his tenacious defense and player development, Coach Thibs has revitalized the Knicks’ identity as a hard-nosed team built to compete.
